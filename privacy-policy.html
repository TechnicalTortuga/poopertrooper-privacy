Privacy Policy for Pooper Trooper

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

Quick Summary: Pooper Trooper is a free game that shows ads to keep it running. We don't collect or store any personal information. Game progress is saved locally on your device. We use Google AdMob for ads and Apple GameCenter for leaderboards.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pooper Trooper! This privacy policy explains how our game ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you play Floppy Turd (the "Game") on iOS devices.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what information is collected, how it's used, and your choices regarding your information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, phone number, or address
  • We do NOT collect your photos, contacts, or location
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites

3. Information Collected by Third Parties

3.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the Game. AdMob may collect and use the following information:

  • Device Identifier: A unique advertising ID assigned to your device
  • Ad Interaction Data: Which ads you view or click
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, screen size
  • App Usage Data: How you use the Game (e.g., time spent playing)

This information is used to show you relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can limit ad tracking in your iOS Settings:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy | AdMob Privacy

3.2 Apple GameCenter (Leaderboards)

If you choose to use GameCenter, Apple collects your GameCenter username and leaderboard scores. We do not have access to your personal GameCenter information.

Learn more: Apple Privacy Policy

4. Game Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored ONLY on your device and is never sent to us:

  • Your game progress (levels completed, high scores)
  • Coins earned and items purchased
  • Game settings (sound, music volume)
  • Unlocked hats and customizations

This data is stored using Apple's secure local storage and is deleted when you delete the app.

5. In-App Purchases

The Game offers an optional in-app purchase to remove ads ("Remove Ads" for $2.00). This purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored if you reinstall the app on the same account.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Floppy Turd is rated 9+ and is suitable for children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children
  • Game progress is stored locally on the device only
  • Ads shown are family-friendly and appropriate for general audiences
  • We have configured AdMob to serve only age-appropriate content

Parents: If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Game data is stored securely on your device using Apple's encryption
  • In-app purchases are protected by Apple's secure payment system
  • We use cryptographic verification to prevent tampering with purchase data

8. Your Privacy Choices

You have control over your privacy:

  • Ad Tracking: Disable tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Remove Ads: Purchase "Remove Ads" to stop seeing advertisements
  • GameCenter: Sign out of GameCenter in iOS Settings if you don't want to use leaderboards
  • Delete Data: Delete the app to remove all locally stored game data

9. International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please note that information may be processed by Google AdMob and Apple in countries where they operate. By using the Game, you consent to this transfer and processing.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your privacy while using Floppy Turd, please contact us:

  • Email: floppyturdthegame@gmail.com
  • GitHub: github.com/TechnicalTortuga/floppyturd-privacy

12. Third-Party Links

This privacy policy applies only to Floppy Turd. The Game may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as ads) that have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

13. California Privacy Rights

California residents: Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to know what personal information is collected and how it is used. Since we do not collect personal information directly, we do not sell personal information. Ad personalization is handled by Google AdMob, which you can opt out of through iOS settings.

14. European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access data collected about you
  • Right to delete your data (delete the app to remove local data)
  • Right to object to ad personalization (disable tracking in iOS settings)

Google AdMob complies with GDPR and will show a consent form to users in the EEA.
